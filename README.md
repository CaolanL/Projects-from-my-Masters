Also included under the data science projects, is a poster we designed and presented on **SaLSa**. In our pre-assigned groups, we had to pick a paper from a list given, to present a poster on. ## 6. Topics in Num. Mathematics A (April 2026)
In this course, we worked in groups to study and explore a chosen topic. We wrote a report on all of our findings and analysis and then presented these findings to our class. Our chosen topic was on *Kernel Ridge Regression* (KRR). In summary, this is a regularised regression problem that can be implemented for various regression or classification data sets and boils down to solving a linear system which includes forming the crucial kernel matrix $K$. This approach becomes computationally infeasible in most scenarios, especially very large datasets. As such, we implemented randomised selection techniques to combat this bottleneck, compared their accuracies as well as runtimes. \
Files attached include the final report, presentation slides, and the required files for our report.
